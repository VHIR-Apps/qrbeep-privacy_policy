Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025  |  Version 2.0

1 Introduction

Welcome to QR Beep Pro ("App"), developed and operated by VHIR ("we", "us", "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

By downloading, installing, or using QR Beep Pro, you agree to the terms described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with any part of this policy, please do not use the App.

🔒 Our Core Promise: QR Beep Pro is designed with privacy-first architecture. We do NOT sell your personal data to third parties, and most of your data stays entirely on your device.

2 Information We Collect

2.1 Information Stored Locally (On Your Device)

The following data is stored only on your device and is never transmitted to our servers:

  • QR codes and barcodes you scan
  • QR codes you generate
  • Scan history and timestamps
  • Items saved in the Secure Vault
  • App preferences and settings
  • Custom logos uploaded for QR generation

2.2 Information Processed Through Firebase

We use Google Firebase for specific features. The following may be processed through Firebase servers:

  • Enterprise VIP login credentials (email and encrypted password)
  • Admin panel configuration data
  • Anonymous usage analytics (via Firebase Analytics)
  • App crash reports for debugging purposes

2.3 Information Collected by Third-Party Services

Our third-party advertising partner (Unity Ads) may collect:

  • Device identifier (advertising ID)
  • Device type and operating system version
  • General location (country/region level, not precise)
  • Ad interaction data (views, clicks)

3 How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide core QR scanning and generation functionality
  • To maintain your scan history and preferences
  • To process in-app purchases and subscription management
  • To display relevant advertisements (free users only)
  • To verify Enterprise VIP license credentials
  • To improve app performance and fix bugs
  • To send important app notifications (with your consent)
  • To manage referral codes and promotional rewards

⚡ Important: We do NOT use your scan data, QR content, or vault items for advertising, marketing, profiling, or any purpose other than providing the service to you.

4 Camera & Device Permissions

QR Beep Pro requires the following device permissions:

Permission Purpose Required?
📷 Camera Scanning QR codes and barcodes in real-time Yes
📁 Storage/Gallery Saving generated QR codes and scanning from gallery Yes
🔐 Biometric Securing the Vault with fingerprint/face authentication Optional
🔦 Flashlight Illumination during scanning in dark environments Optional
📶 Internet Ads, Firebase sync, license verification, purchases Yes
📳 Vibration Haptic feedback on successful scan Optional

Camera images are processed entirely on your device in real-time. We do not capture, store, transmit, or analyze camera images beyond the QR/barcode decoding process.

5 Data Security

We implement the following security measures to protect your data:

  • All local data is stored using encrypted Hive database on your device
  • Vault items are protected with device-level biometric authentication
  • Firebase communications use industry-standard SSL/TLS encryption
  • VIP credentials are stored in secure Firestore with access controls
  • We do not store any passwords in plain text
  • Auto-clipboard clearing is available for sensitive scanned content
  • Secure session mode auto-locks after period of inactivity

🛡️ AI Phishing Shield: Our AI-powered security feature analyzes scanned URLs locally on your device to detect potential phishing threats. No URL data is sent to external servers for this analysis.

6 Third-Party Services

QR Beep Pro integrates with the following third-party services:

6.1 Google Firebase

Used for app configuration, analytics, crash reporting, and enterprise authentication. Firebase is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

6.2 Unity Ads

Used to display advertisements to free-tier users. Unity Ads may collect device information and advertising identifiers. See Unity's Privacy Policy.

6.3 Google Play Billing

Used to process in-app purchases and subscriptions. Payment information is handled entirely by Google Play and is never accessible to us. See Google's Privacy Policy.

6.4 Google Play Store

The App is distributed through Google Play Store, which may collect installation and usage data as per Google's policies.

7 Advertising

QR Beep Pro displays advertisements to users who have not purchased a Pro subscription. Our advertising is powered by Unity Ads.

  • Ad Types: Banner ads, interstitial (full-screen) ads, and rewarded video ads
  • Pro Users: All advertisements are completely removed for Pro subscribers
  • Ad Data: Unity Ads may collect device identifiers and interaction data
  • Ad Sound: Can be muted through app settings or admin configuration
  • Ad Frequency: Controlled remotely through our admin panel

You may opt out of personalized advertising through your device's advertising settings:

  • Android: Settings → Privacy → Ads → Delete Advertising ID

8 Subscriptions & Purchases

QR Beep Pro offers the following subscription plans:

  • Weekly Plan — Auto-renewable weekly subscription
  • Monthly Plan — Auto-renewable monthly subscription
  • Yearly Plan — Auto-renewable yearly subscription

All purchases are processed through Google Play Store. We do not have access to your payment card details, bank information, or billing address.

Subscription management, cancellation, and refunds are handled through your Google Play account settings.

9 Children's Privacy

QR Beep Pro is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age.

If you believe that a child under 13 has provided personal information through our App, please contact us immediately at vhirsupport@gmail.com, and we will take steps to delete such information.

10 Data Retention & Deletion

Since most data is stored locally on your device:

  • Scan History: Retained until you manually delete it or clear the app cache
  • Vault Items: Retained until you manually remove them
  • App Preferences: Retained until you reset settings or uninstall the app
  • Firebase Data: VIP credentials retained until license expiry or manual deletion
  • Analytics Data: Automatically anonymized and aggregated by Firebase

To delete all your data: Uninstall QR Beep Pro from your device, or use the "Reset Settings" and "Clear Cache" options in the app settings.

11 Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: You can view all your data within the app (history, vault, settings)
  • Deletion: You can delete your scan history, vault items, and cache at any time
  • Portability: You can share or export your QR codes from the app
  • Opt-out: You can disable analytics, sounds, haptic feedback, and other features
  • Ad Preferences: You can upgrade to Pro to remove all advertisements
  • Account Deletion: VIP users can request account deletion via email

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make significant changes:

  • We will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
  • We may notify you through an in-app notification
  • Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy

We recommend reviewing this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
